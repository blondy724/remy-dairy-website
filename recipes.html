Breakfast

Golden Butter Croissants

Prep Time: 20 min | Bake Time: 25 min

Flaky and buttery croissants made with our rich butter.

  • Ingredients: Butter, flour, yeast, sugar, salt...
  • Instructions:
    1. Prepare the dough by mixing dry and wet ingredients.
    2. Layer with butter, fold, and roll out several times.
    3. Shape, let rise, and bake until golden brown.

Tip: Serve warm for the ultimate experience!

Desserts

Cheesy Chocolate Brownies

Prep Time: 15 min | Bake Time: 30 min

A delightful twist on classic brownies with a cheesy layer.

  • Ingredients: Cream cheese, butter, cocoa powder, sugar, eggs...
  • Instructions:
    1. Mix brownie batter and spread half in a pan.
    2. Blend cream cheese with sugar and spread over the batter.
    3. Layer the remaining batter and bake until firm.

Beverages

Refreshing Yogurt Smoothie

Prep Time: 5 min | Chill Time: 5 min

A creamy drink packed with flavor and made with our fresh yogurt.

  • Ingredients: Yogurt, honey, fruits (e.g., bananas, berries), ice cubes...
  • Instructions:
    1. Blend yogurt, honey, and fruits until smooth.
    2. Add ice and blend again for a chilled treat.

Tip: Use frozen fruits for a thicker texture.
